## Core Team

### Sébastien Mosser

![seb's picture]({{ site.url }}{{ site.baseurl }}/assets/images/people/seb.jpg){: .align-right}

[Sébastien](https://mosser.github.io) is an Associate Professor of Software Engineering at McMaster University. He is currently Associate Chair of the Computing and Software department, and Associate Director of McSCert, McMaster Centre for Software Certification. His research interests are related to software engineering, software composition, domain-specific languages and modelling at large. In addition, he applies his research to various domains such as machine learning pipelines, distributed systems, DevOps and safety.
