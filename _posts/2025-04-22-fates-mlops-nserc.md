On April 22, 2025, the FATES-MLOps project was awarded $270,000 by the _Natural Sciences and Engineering Research Council of Canada_ (NSERC) to work on _Incorporating FATES Principles in Continuous Development of ML-Integrated Systems_. 
{: .text-justify }

This funding complements the one already secured by the French part of the consortium, to focus on the follwing question: **"Can we systematically and progressively include justifications related to the FATES (_Fairness, Accountability, Transparency, Ethics, and Security_) building and operating of a software system integrating Machine Learning?**"
{: .text-justify }

Project website: [http://fates-mlops.org/](http://fates-mlops.org/)
